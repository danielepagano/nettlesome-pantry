Nettlesome Pantry migration guide

Use this page with the converter on GitHub Pages. Your Squarespace CSV files stay on your computer; they are not uploaded to GitHub.

Before you start

  • Export fresh files from Squarespace right before import. Do not reuse old exports.
  • Do not email exports, paste them into GitHub issues, or commit them to git.
  • This tool converts products and contacts only. Orders, reviews, theme pages, shipping, taxes, and payments are handled separately in Shopify.

Step 1: Export from Squarespace

  1. Products: in Squarespace, open Products and export the catalog CSV.
  2. Contacts: open Contacts, choose Export all contacts, and save the profiles-style CSV.

Step 2: Convert on GitHub Pages

  1. Open the converter.
  2. Upload one Squarespace CSV at a time.
  3. Leave export type on automatic unless the tool cannot tell products from contacts.
  4. For contacts, keep the default country as US unless a row already has a shipping country.
  5. For products, set the Shopify storefront URL to your real store domain or your-store.myshopify.com so product links in descriptions can be rewritten.
  6. Read the summary, warnings, and link notes before you download.
  7. Download shopify-products.csv and shopify-customers.csv.

Contacts without a shipping address are normal for newsletter signups and marketing contacts who never ordered. Only rows without an email are skipped.

Step 3: Import into Shopify

  1. Products → Import. On the first pass, leave publish to all sales channels off if you want to review before going live.
  2. Customers → Import.
  3. Spot-check prices, inventory, variants, images, and a few customer addresses.

Step 4: Fix the items this CSV does not cover

  • Gift card: recreate it manually in Shopify. Adding or updating gift card products, Creating and selling gift cards.
  • Bundle-style products such as the Three Pack: rebuild them in Shopify so customers can pick soaps with real inventory links. Product bundles, Adding variants.
  • Sales: set compare-at and sale prices in Shopify. The converter imports the regular Squarespace Price when On Sale is No, even if an old sale price column is still present. Setting sale prices for products, Discounts.

Step 5: Finish the move in Shopify

Complete the rest of the migration in Shopify: theme, shipping, taxes, payments, a test order, your domain, and URL redirects. Squarespace migration guide.
